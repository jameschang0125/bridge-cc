\subsection{high level t/o style}

We use double very "elasticly":

\begin{itemize}
    \setlength\itemsep{0pt}
    \item at 4H+, tend to show a strong bal with good defensive tricks
    \item however, may also be hands that promises transferable strength, allowing partner to bid with good shape. An example is \texttt{-.AQJxx.Axxx.KTxx}. With stronger values, you will bid \texttt{4N} for t/o.
    \item X to 4H tend to be more t/o-ish, compare to X to 4S+
\end{itemize}
